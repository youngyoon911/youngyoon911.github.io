## About Me

I am a M.S. student in the [Graduate School of Data Science](https://gsds.snu.ac.kr/) at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by [Prof. Joonseok Lee](http://www.joonseok.net/home.html) at the [Visual Information Processing (VIP) Lab](http://viplab.snu.ac.kr/).

**I am interested in learning the world from visual experience through generative modeling.** My current research focuses on video diffusion and flow models, particularly controllable video generation. Recently, I have addressed the fundamental failure modes in motion customization and developed theoretically grounded approaches based on stochastic optimal control. Looking ahead, I aim to leverage the rich representations learned by generative models to build persistent world models that enable interactive, world-consistent, and long-horizon generation.
